Unit 2: Introduction to Firewalls

Firewalls is a core concept in modern cybersecurity. In this unit, we discuss the basic definition, why it matters, and how it is used in real systems.

Subtopics:
- Firewalls definition and core ideas
- Common use cases of Firewalls
- Challenges and limitations

Short explanation:
Firewalls are filters that decide which network traffic is allowed or blocked based on rules.

Example questions:
1. Define Firewalls and explain its purpose.
2. Give two real-world scenarios where Firewalls is used.
